Delicious and refreshing fruit cocktail

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. Strawberries
  • ½ cup Blueberries
  • ½ cup Raspberries
  • 3 Kiwi
  • 2 Bananas
  • 1 cup Pineapple
  • 2 Mangoes
  • 4 tsp. Honey
  • ½ cup Chopped Walnuts or Halved Almonds
  • ½ cup Water

Ashta:

  • 6 cups Whole Milk
  • 3 tb. Lemon Juice
  • 2 tsp. Rose Water
  • 1 tsp. Orange Blossom water
