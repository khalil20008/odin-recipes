Lentils Recipe

Delicious lentils recipe!

Hello friends, in this recipe you will learn how to make delicious and most importantly easy and quick lentils.

i promise they dont take much work!! so i hope you leave this site with some extra delicous knowledge that you can put to use on your day to day life (:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of lentils
  • 2 cups of water filled all the way
  • 1 to 2 big spoons of butter or oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of pepper
  • 2 to 4 teaspoons of your favorite spice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 half an onion

Steps:

  1. first wash the lentils under running water for 1 minute
  2. on your pan add the oil or butter and turn the gas on low heat
  3. add the garlic to cook
  4. after the garlic is cooked add the lentils
  5. add all the spices you prepared and mix them together
  6. after everything is really mixed, wait for 1 minute
  7. add the 2 full cups of water to the pan
  8. now turn the gas on high heat until the water boils
  9. after the water is boiling, turn it to low heat
  10. this is when you will use the onion! cook the onion on a separate pan
  11. after the onion is cooked and looks soft and a bit darker,add it on top of the lentils
  12. now wait for around 20-30 minutes until all the water is gone
  13. most important step!! ENJOY, I HOPE IT'S DELICIOUS
